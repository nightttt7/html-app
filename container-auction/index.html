Container Auction / 集装箱竞拍站

The game now runs day by day: each day you may buy exactly one container or skip, sell warehouse items one by one, choose at most one extra reward, then pay overnight costs and the next license fee before the next day begins. 现在改成按天推进：每天只能从几只柜子里选一只买下，或直接跳过；卖货是逐件卖；卖完后还能选一条额外奖励，然后再进入下一天。

Daily Auction / 今日竞拍

Pick one lot or skip entirely. After the auction you can sell any warehouse items individually, then claim at most one extra reward before ending the day. 每天只能买一只柜子，或者选择不买；卖货阶段可以逐件卖，卖完再挑一条额外奖励。

Phase / 当前阶段

Auction / 竞拍

Choose one container or skip the auction. 先从今天的几只柜子里选一只，或者直接跳过。

Extra Reward / 额外奖励

Choose at most one / 最多选一条

These bonuses can be free or cost cash. Rewards affect your next day unless noted otherwise. 奖励有的免费有的要花钱，通常会影响下一天。

Lose condition: when the next day starts, storage and license are deducted automatically; if the license leaves you broke, the run ends. 胜利条件是现金先冲到 ¥10,000；进入下一天会自动扣仓储和牌照费，扣完牌照后没钱就直接失败。

Warehouse & Log / 仓库与经营记录

Opened goods go into your own warehouse. Sell item by item when today's offer looks good, or hold them and pay cheap storage for a later spike. 开出来的货物都会进自己的仓库；你可以今天逐件卖，也可以付比较便宜的仓储费等以后更好的价格。
